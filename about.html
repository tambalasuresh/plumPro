About Us

Turnkey PHE contractors

PlumbPro Tech Private Limited is a Bangalore-based turnkey PHE contracting firm delivering comprehensive mechanical, plumbing, sub soil drainage, water feature, and swimming pool solutions since 2022.

With 3+ active projects and an order book of Rs. 2+ crores, our engineering-led team executes large-scale, technically complex works across residential, commercial, IT park, mall, and hotel developments.

Trusted by leading developers including Puravankara, Bagmane, Embassy, and many more, PlumbPro Tech is recognized for precision, quality, timely execution, and dependable delivery under one roof.

Leadership Message

At PTPL, I am pleased to present the PlumbPro Tech Pvt. Ltd. SOP Manual.

I am proud of the achievements of our employees for their commitment and dedication to the company. At the same time, I recognize that a large family should be bonded by a common vision and driven by similar values. All employees need to be familiar with the company's goals, culture, and values. They should be clear about expectations and aware of their own responsibilities. This will help us work more productively, become better team players, enjoy what we do, and above all, provide service that meets or exceeds our customers' expectations.

Our admin philosophy is guided by the same belief: we strive for the best possible fit between employees, their roles, and our projects. It is important to us that everyone enjoys their work. For this reason, we screen candidates carefully in two areas: competence and attitude. When there is a clear fit, we offer candidates strong growth prospects and a motivating remuneration package. People who do exceptional work are rewarded well. In this way, we maintain the true PTPL spirit: doing excellent work together and enjoying it.

I am confident that this SOP Manual will provide useful guidance, build shared understanding, and help every member become part of a strong team.

I look forward to your commitment, hard work, and team spirit as we build a bright future for all members of PTPL.

Krishnaraj T Chairman and Managing Director
